About Burgers and History Special.....

A burger is a sandwich of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, typically beef, placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. The patty is usually seasoned with salt and pepper, and sometimes other spices, and then grilled, fried, or broiled until it is cooked to the desired level of doneness.

The vegetarian burgers found in the market today are not only considered as healthy alternatives but also more delicious than their meat counterparts.

The United States is undoubtedly the country most famous for burgers with companies like McDonald's, Burger King, and Wendy's taking the magic of burgers to every nook and corner of the world.
